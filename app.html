Care & Tips
Glass containers · Cleaning · Troubleshooting
Glass container care
🧊
Avoid sudden temperature changes
Never add cold food to a hot container or vice versa. Let glass cool to room temp before placing in water, sink, or dishwasher.
🛡️
Handles & base are permanent
The heat-protectant base and easy-grip handles must never be removed. Stop using the container if they become damaged.
⚠️
Inspect before every use
If a container becomes chipped, cracked, or deeply scratched, discard it immediately — do not use. Dispose in standard waste, not recycling.
🍽️
Safe to put on any surface
The heat-protectant base means containers can go straight onto any countertop during or after cooking.

Troubleshooting
📺
"ADD POT" on the screen
Container not installed correctly
›
The glass container isn't seated properly. Make sure the modular base is in the correct position for your container size (2.5QT = High, 4QT = Middle, 6QT = Low), then push the container all the way back until it clicks into place.

🍳
Food not cooking evenly
Browning or texture issues
›
Arrange ingredients in a single even layer with no overlapping. Use the crisper plate so air can circulate underneath. Shake or flip halfway through the cook time using the easy-grip handles or a silicone-tipped utensil.

🔥
Food is burning or overcooked
Check progress frequently
›
Check food frequently and remove when your desired level of brownness is achieved. You can adjust time and temperature at any time during cooking — press Temp or Time and use the dial. Remove food immediately when the timer ends.

💨
Food blowing around inside
Lightweight ingredients
›
Lightweight foods can blow around from the fan. Use toothpicks to secure loose items like sandwich tops. For breaded foods, ensure breadcrumbs are firmly pressed on — coat flour first, then egg, then breadcrumbs.

📱
Screen went black
Standby mode
›
The unit has gone into standby mode. Simply press the Power/Start button to wake it up. This is normal behaviour during idle periods.
❗
"E" error on screen
Malfunction detected
›
The unit is not functioning properly. Stop use immediately and contact Ninja Customer Service at 1-877-646-5288 or visit ninjakitchen.com/support.
